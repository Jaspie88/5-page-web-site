Maurice Lidman

Owner and Head Trainer

ML Strength is a Brisbane based company with clubs at Ashgrove, Brisbane City, Chermside, Graceville, and Westlake. We believe fitness comes first and keeping fit and improving your health is not just a fad - it's a way of life. Our trainers are all Les Mills accredited instructors with Bachelor Degrees in Human Movements. We pride ourselves on our family-operated business with an old school feel and a new school attitude. Our gyms are OPEN 24/7, with staff always available to help you achieve your fitness goals.
